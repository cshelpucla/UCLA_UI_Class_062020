
Definition of agile

Agile project management is an iterative development methodology that values human communication and feedback, 
adapting to change, and producing working results.

Agile is iterative, meaning that it is done in pieces (sprints), with each sprint building and improving off the 
lessons from the previous sprint. This is where that term Scrum comes into play. What is Scrum? Scrum methodology 
is a workflow framework made up of sprints and reviews used to promote Agile project management.
